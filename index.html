Flagship

MedLync

MedLync is OmniTech’s flagship healthcare operations platform, purpose-built to elevate how Spanish healthcare providers manage their workforce. By connecting clinics, hospitals, and surgical centers with qualified practitioners in a single intelligent ecosystem, MedLync streamlines talent sourcing, shift scheduling, and regulatory compliance—empowering providers to operate more efficiently with analytics at the core.

SupplySense

SupplySense is an AI-driven inventory solution that helps healthcare facilities better manage medical supplies. It predicts usage to cut waste, prevents shortages with real-time alerts, and improves purchasing through smarter ordering patterns. With built-in analytics and simple integration, SupplySense enables clinics, hospitals, and surgical centers to maintain proper stock, control costs, and support continuous patient care.

InsightHub

InsightHub is a unified data and analytics layer that centralizes operational, clinical, and administrative information to eliminate system silos. By connecting platforms and standardizing data into a full 360° view, InsightHub supports faster and more informed decisions. With automated ingestion, real-time dashboards, and built-in analytics, it becomes a core tool for planning, performance improvement, and operational efficiency across healthcare providers.
